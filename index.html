Account & Data Deletion

What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently remove all data associated with your account from our servers:

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Character progress, levels, and cultivation points
  • Leaderboard entries (all-time, weekly, monthly)
  • Purchase history and currency balances (Gold & Silver)
  • Cosmetics, inventory, and equipped items
  • Gameplay session logs
  • Redeemed codes and daily reward history

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. If you play again after deletion, you will start with a fresh guest account.

How to request deletion

1
From inside the app — open your Profile and tap Delete Account. Your account and all data will be removed immediately.

This is the fastest way and takes effect instantly.

2
If you no longer have the app — send us an email with the address you registered with, and we will delete your account manually.
Send deletion requests to lonelyscrub8@gmail.com Send Request
Email requests are processed within 7 business days. You will receive a confirmation once your data has been removed.

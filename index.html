Jianghu: Sword & Zither

"Where the zither becomes your sword." 「以琴为剑，以音为道。」

For generations, the Xuanyin Sect has called Mount Jade Mist its home, cultivating the arts of music and inner strength. Within its walls, music is not merely art — every melody becomes a weapon, every note a testament to years of cultivation. Guardian of this tradition is Master Mo Wenyin, keeper of the sect's centuries-old legacy.

But beneath the calm of Jianghu, old conflicts stir. Wei Chengzhi, once a disciple of the Xuanyin Sect, returns bearing the forbidden Rending Resonance Technique — a martial art designed to unravel the sect's very foundation. Three infamous factions answer his call, and together they march on Mount Jade Mist.

Choose your path. Become Han Yuan or Yun Lan, defend the Xuanyin Sect, and let your melody shape the fate of Jianghu.

数百年来，玄音门立足于玉雾山，以音律修心，以内力悟武，代代相传，从未断绝。在这里，音律不仅是艺术，更是武学之道——琴音可凝聚真气，旋律可化作攻守之势。守护这份传承的，是玄音门掌门莫闻音。

然而，江湖暗流涌动，一股黑暗势力悄然崛起。昔日玄音门弟子魏承志，携禁术《裂音诀》卷土重来，专克玄音门音律真气，且此番并非孤身而至。三大邪派随其兵临玉雾山，一场浩劫即将降临。

化身寒渊或云岚，执琴迎敌，守护玄音门，在这场风云际会中，谱写属于你的江湖传奇。
